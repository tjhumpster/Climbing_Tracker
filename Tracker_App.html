Skill wall
Positioning & movement quality
Skill
Warm-up (0–15 min)▾
Juggy V1–V2 laps. Climb deliberately slow — treat each move like a drill.
Shoulder CARs: 10 circles each arm. Wrist figure-8s.
Positioning drill: hip-to-wall (15–45 min)▸
The cue: Before each reach, your hip on the reaching side should already be flagged or twisted toward the wall. Not after you're stuck — before.
Pick 6 problems V3–V4. On each one, pause at every hold and consciously flag or twist before pulling. Climb slow enough that you can narrate it.
If a move feels hard, ask: are my hips square to the wall? If yes, that's the problem. Twist in.
3-point rule (45–70 min)▸
One limb moves at a time. Other 3 must be stable and intentional before you move.
Pick 4 problems. If you barn-door or cut feet — restart from the previous hold, no penalty, just redo.
Goal is controlled movement, not speed.
Free climb (70–90 min)▸
V4–V5 free climbing. Try to apply the hip-in habit without thinking about it.
Note any problem where you had to consciously correct your positioning.
